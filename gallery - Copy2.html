In-Engine Creation
Assault city

This project represents my work within Unreal Engine 4, where I delved into the realm of first-person shooter (FPS) game development. The focal point of this endeavor is the meticulously designed map, which has been strategically crafted to provide an initial advantage to the police team by letting them take control of the sites. This advantage plays a pivotal role in balancing the odds against the terrorists, who often push together. The map offers players a diverse range of choke points, facilitating tactical rotations and creating a dynamic battlefield. With an abundance of angles strategically placed throughout, players must exercise caution and precision to outsmart their opponents. For a more in-depth look at the design choices, please watch the accompanying video above.

Undertaking a project on my own, I set out to create a modern house in a dry climate backdrop. I highlighted the arid surroundings through a unique roof design and other architectural choices.
